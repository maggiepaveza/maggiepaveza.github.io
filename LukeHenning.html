LUKE HENNING





Full Name: Luke Henning
Age: 22
Hometown: San Diego, CA
Height: 6'2"
Favorite Color: Green
Role/Instrument: Bass Guitar, Vocals


Meet the Members.

Taylor Barnes
Tyler Page
Luke Henning
Jackson Short

Q: If we came to your house, what would you cook us?
A: Paninis. The sandwich is the king of all foods!

Q: If you guys were stuck on an island and had no food, who would you eat first?
A: Taylor without a doubt. He lacks the finance skills we need to build our island banking empire so we can properly hide rich people's money.

Q: What’s your perfect pizza?
A: Lots of meat (no fish) and veggies with goat cheese to top it off.

Q: Growing up, what was your favorite halloween costume?
A: My cardboard Megatron costume in first grade was probably as good as it gets.

Q: Did you ever have music lessons?
A: 6 years. You can never learn too much.

Q: How many ties do you own?
A: I went to Catholic school so I had a pretty extensive collection, probably 20-30 if you count the ones my brother stole.

Q: To people who haven’t listened to your music before, why should they?
A: Why not? Our music is made to be accessible and dancy. We want the whole crowd to be on their feet.

Q: Who’s your biggest musical inspiration?
A: There may be a lot of haters out there but I think Sting (Police years) is one of the greatest bassist/singers of all time. Nobody plays like he does. The pure combination of uniqueness and skill really sets him apart.

Q: Who writes the songs and what are they about?
A: Taylor writes songs and I don't think even he knows what he is singing sometimes.

Q: Which of your songs is your favorite and why?
A: My favorite of our songs is probably "The Bonnie Situation" (yet to be released). It's one of my favorite live ones and it really fun to play.

Q: What quote do you live by/what’s your favorite quote?
A: “Something coming back from the dead was almost always bad news. Movies taught me that. For every one Jesus you get a million zombies.” ― David Wong, (John Dies at the End)
