OUR STORY





"These boys are amazingly talented and always smartly dressed! Their music makes me smile and want to dance."

-Jackson's Mom

A group of classy, eccentric guys coming together to gift the world of their music.


The Feds. are a high energy indie-rock band originating from Northern California. They recently transplanted down to San Diego for school and work reasons. Not unlike Derek Zoolander, being really really ridiculously good looking is a way of life, especially if you throw in a good suit. But, really, in all seriousness they like having a good time just like everyone else.
